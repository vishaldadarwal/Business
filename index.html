Analytics and company strategy

How business analysis can support the implementation of the company's strategy

Business analusis, or Business intelligence, is the collection of data and their reliable analysis, used in the company's business activities.The basic goal of BI is to provide information and know edge necessary to make decisions and shape beneficial business effects.

In practice, conducting business analusis boils down to the use of intelligent IT systems that enable the collection of huge amounts of various data, their processing and presentation in the from of key indicators, tables or charts.

All these data are presented in the form of interactive dashboards. The more complete knowledge, the more effective decisions - that is why using business intelligence tools is so important today.
